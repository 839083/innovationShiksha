#02 development

Development is the essence of software engineering, driving problem-solving, innovation, and societal impact. As developers, continual learning and effective collaboration are key, ensuring high-quality software delivery. Overall, development is integral, offering opportunities for innovation and positive change.




webdevelopement

Web development is essential for software developers, offering opportunities to create user-friendly interfaces, innovate, and deploy solutions globally. Proficiency in web development enhances versatility and enables developers to tackle a variety of projects, from simple websites to complex applications, in today's digital age.



App Developement

#03 hackathon

Participating in hackathons as a student is invaluable. It offers hands-on problem-solving, fosters creativity, and provides networking opportunities. It also builds essential skills like teamwork, communication, and time management, crucial for future success.

What is hackathon

A hackathon is a brief, intensive event where programmers and designers collaborate to tackle challenges and develop solutions within a tight timeframe, usually a day or a weekend. It fosters innovation, teamwork, and networking, often leading to the creation of prototypes or products with real-world applications.



hackathon player

Hackathon players value these events for hands-on learning, creativity, and networking. They thrive in the intense atmosphere, fostering innovation and quick problem-solving. These gatherings provide opportunities to showcase skills, make meaningful contributions, and connect with peers who share their passion for technology.

#04 internships

An internship is a short-term work experience for students or recent graduates to gain practical skills and insights in a specific field. It provides hands-on learning, professional development, and networking opportunities to explore career paths.

importance of internship

Internships in the second year offer invaluable real-world experience, helping students apply theoretical knowledge, develop professional skills, and explore career options early on. They provide networking opportunities, mentorship, and potential employment prospects, serving as a crucial step towards career readiness.



internship experience

To find an internship, research companies in your field, use job search platforms like LinkedIn, attend career fairs, and network with professionals. Tailor your resume and cover letter, and consider reaching out directly to companies. Be proactive in following up on applications and showcasing your enthusiasm and qualifications.
